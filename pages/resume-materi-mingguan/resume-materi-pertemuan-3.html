Resume Pertemuan 3

What are the benefits of IT Governance?

• Alignment of business and IT strategies.
• Greater efficiencies, increased support and reduced costs when implementing change.
• Effective risk management and regulatory compliance.
• Effective and efficient use of IT resources for asset utilization, and business growth and flexibility.
• Improved culture in adherence to company policies and procedures.
• Improved communication of the role of IT in achieving business results and the capability to meet business needs.
• Improved IT performance measurement capabilities.

IT Governance Assessment Approach

1. Identify key contacts within the Organization’s business organization.
2. Conduct interviews with each contact on an individual basis.
3. Allow for free-form discussion to understand the areas of greatest concern in IT.
4. Obtained and review supporting documentation and evidence.
5. Information obtained is assessed to evaluate the maturity level of each of the IT governance capabilities which are defined in the IT Governance Maturity Model* (GMM).
6. Provide recommendations and track remediation.
